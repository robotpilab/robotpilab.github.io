MotionBits: Video Segmentation through Motion-Level Analysis of Rigid Bodies
MotionBits introduces a novel motion-based segmentation concept defining the smallest unit through kinematic spatial twist equivalence. We contribute the MoRiBo benchmark for rigid-body segmentation and a learning-free method that outperforms state-of-the-art embodied perception approaches by 37.3%.
Howard H. Qian, Kejia Ren, Yu Xiang, Vicente Ordonez, Kaiyu Hang

ManipulationNet: An Infrastructure for Benchmarking Real-World Robot Manipulation with Physical Skill Challenges and Embodied Multimodal Reasoning
ManipulationNet is a global infrastructure for benchmarking real-world robotic manipulation, offering standardized hardware kits and a unified software client for distributed evaluation across physical skills and embodied reasoning tracks.
